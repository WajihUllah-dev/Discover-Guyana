Guyana

Guyana is a land of breathtaking landscapes, rich biodiversity, and adventure. Whether you seek stunning waterfalls or legendary mountain hikes, we offer unforgettable experiences to two of the country's most iconic locations: Kaieteur Falls and Mount Roraima.

Things to do in Guyana

Kaieteur Falls

Kaieteur Falls -One of the world's most powerful waterfalls, located in a pristine rainforest.Nestled in the heart of Kaieteur National Park, Kaieteur Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder. Plunging 741 feet in a single drop—nearly five times the height of Niagara Falls—this waterfall offers a truly raw and majestic experience.

Mount Roraima

Mount Roraima -A mysterious tabletop mountain with unique landscapes and views over three countries.Towering at 9,200 feet, Mount Roraima is one of the oldest geological formations on Earth, straddling the borders of Guyana, Venezuela, and Brazil. Often shrouded in mist, this mysterious tabletop mountain inspired Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s novel The Lost World.
