Kaieteur Falls – The Jewel of Guyana

Nestled in the heart of Kaieteur National Park, Kaieteur Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder. Plunging 741 feet in a single drop—nearly five times the height of Niagara Falls—this waterfall offers a truly raw and majestic experience.

Why Visit Kaieteur Falls?

  • Untamed Wilderness: No crowds, just nature at its finest.
  • Incredible Wildlife: Home to the golden frog and cock-of-the-rock bird.
  • Scenic Beauty: The towering cascade and misty cliffs create a surreal setting.

Activities at Kaieteur Falls

  • Scenic Hiking Trails: Explore rainforest paths with panoramic waterfall views.
  • Birdwatching & Photography: Capture the vibrant flora and fauna.
  • Guided Tours: Learn about the indigenous Patamona people and local legends.

How to Get There?

Kaieteur Falls is accessible via a 1-hour scenic flight from Georgetown. Guyana Airlines offers chartered flights with stunning aerial views before landing near the falls.
